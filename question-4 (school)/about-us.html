Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate educators and visionary parents, Lumos Academy began as a small learning collective with a bold mission — to reimagine education for the 21st century. What started in a single classroom has grown into a thriving educational community, known for its student-centered approach, innovative curriculum, and commitment to nurturing every learner's potential.

Over the years, Lumos Academy has embraced a philosophy that blends academic excellence with creativity, critical thinking, and global awareness. From pioneering project-based learning to integrating technology in meaningful ways, we’ve always focused on preparing students not just for exams — but for life. Today, Lumos Academy continues to grow, serving students from diverse backgrounds, while staying true to its founding belief: that every child deserves a place where their light can shine.
